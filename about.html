Our Journey

A Decade and a Half of Growth

From our first office to serving thousands of clients, every milestone reflects our commitment to the communities we protect.

2009

Founded in Long Island City

Ventus Insurance Agency opens its doors in Queens, NY, with a mission to bring honest, accessible insurance to the local community. We started with a small team of 3 dedicated agents.

2012

Expanding Our Product Portfolio

We launched Commercial Truck Insurance and Business Insurance lines, responding to the growing needs of New York's entrepreneurs and fleet operators.

2016

Bilingual Service Launch

We officially launched full bilingual (English & Spanish) client services — opening our doors wider to the Latino community across Queens and Brooklyn.

2019

1,000+ Active Clients Milestone

A landmark year — we celebrated protecting over 1,000 active clients and expanded our office space in Long Island City to accommodate our growing team of 12 licensed professionals.

2022

Digital Transformation

We invested in digital tools to streamline quoting, policy management, and claims support — making insurance faster and more accessible for all our clients.

Today

5,000+ Clients Strong & Growing

Today, Ventus Insurance Agency proudly serves over 5,000 clients across New York with a dedicated team, a full suite of 5 insurance products, and a 98% client satisfaction rate.
