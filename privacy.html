Have Questions?

At Ventus Agency LLC, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you visit our website, use our services, or communicate with us by phone, email, or SMS.

By accessing or using our website, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

01

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information from you:

Personal Information

Your name, phone number, email address, and mailing address when you contact us, request a quote, or complete a form.

Insurance Information

Details related to your insurance needs, policy preferences, or claims (if applicable).

02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide requested services
  • Process insurance quotes, applications, and policy renewals
  • Send appointment reminders, billing notifications, or service updates
  • Improve our website, customer service, and communication processes
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

03

Sharing of Information

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With licensed insurance carriers and authorized business partners solely for the purpose of providing insurance services you request.
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process.

Mobile Opt-in, SMS Consent, and phone numbers collected for SMS communication purposes will not be shared with any third party or affiliates for marketing purposes.

04

SMS Communications

If you have provided consent, Ventus Agency LLC may send you text messages related to appointment reminders, billing updates, or customer service.

Opt-Out Anytime

Reply STOP to any message or contact us directly to unsubscribe from SMS communications.

Carrier Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier.

For full details, please review our SMS Terms & Conditions.

05

Data Retention & Security

Data Protection

We maintain appropriate administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Retention Period

Your information is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law.

06

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze website traffic.

Cookies improve navigation and personalize your experience
You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time
Disabling cookies may limit certain features of our website
